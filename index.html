Champagne Crust


This is a delicious and simple old recipe that dates from the 1930's, it was handed down from my wifes grandmother
The original recipe calls for stewed apples to be used, but we subsitute canned fruit instead.

List of ingredients

  • 1 large can of Peaches in natural juice
    Drain off most of the juice, this can be used in your favourite cocktails later.
  • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
  • 60g of Butter
  • 1 Cup of Self Raising Flour
  • 1/2 Cup of Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 or 2 teaspoons of Vanilla Essence to taste

Method

  1. Put the fruit in a saucepan and heat
  2. Mix the butter and sugar to a creamy consistency
  3. Add the egg,milk and vanilla essence
  4. mix well
  5. Add the sifted flour
  6. Stir until the mix had a batter like consistency
  7. Bring the fruit to the boil and place in the baking dish
  8. Pour the batter over the fruit(which should be boiling)
  9. Bake in a moderate oven 375F or 180C for around 20 minutes,
    or until the crust is golden brown

Enjoy hot with your choice of cream, icecream or custard
It's even fabulous as a cold desert
